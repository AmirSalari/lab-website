  • Opportunity

    We have graduate and undergraduate student researchers, visiting students and scholars, and collaborators from both industry and academia all over the world, closely working together on research topics that we believe to be useful to the society. Our research is mainly supported by USA National Science Foundation.

  • Our Research

    We conduct research on a broad range of topics related to computer and communication networks, helping transform the current best-effort Internet to a more reliable, available, and secure information infrastructure for all kinds of communication activities.
